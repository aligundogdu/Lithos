### ğŸ¯ Gameplay Mechanics

#### Production Loop
1. **Accept Orders:** Accept orders from customers
2. **Acquire Materials:** Purchase materials from the market
3. **Assign Workers:** Assign appropriate workers to the order
4. **Production:** Let your workers complete the product
5. **Deliver:** Earn rewards and reputation

#### Progression
- **Reputation:** Each completed order earns reputation
- **Rank:** Unlock new ranks by reaching certain reputation levels
- **Material Unlocking:** Higher ranks provide access to more valuable materials

#### Economy
- **Income:** Earn Drachma from completed orders
- **Expenses:** Worker salaries and tax payments
- **Investment:** Spend on tools, upgrades, and consultants
